Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) for Order Processing

Department: Sales & Fulfillment

Scope:

This SOP applies to all customer orders placed through the online platform, covering order confirmation, processing, packaging, and delivery. It ensures smooth coordination between departments involved in fulfilling orders.

Responsibilities

  • Customer Support Team: Confirms orders, handles customer inquiries, and updates order status.
  • Inventory Team: Checks product availability and updates stock levels.
  • Packing & Shipping Team: Ensures correct and secure packaging of orders.
  • Delivery Team: Handles the safe and timely delivery of orders.

Procedure

Step 1: Order Placement

  • Customers place orders through the website or mobile app.
  • The system generates an automatic confirmation email/SMS.
  • Payment is processed (online or cash-on-delivery).

Step 2: Order Verification

  • The system verifies stock availability.
  • If in stock, the order is approved.
  • If out of stock, the customer is notified, and alternatives are offered.

Step 3: Order Processing

  • The warehouse team picks the items.
  • Eggs and chicks are packed securely to prevent damage.
  • Meat is stored in temperature-controlled packaging.
  • Packages are labeled with order details.

Step 4: Shipping & Delivery

  • Orders are assigned to the delivery team.
  • Tracking details are shared with the customer.
  • Delivery personnel ensure timely and safe delivery.

Step 5: Post-Delivery Follow-up

  • Customer support follows up for feedback.
  • Complaints are recorded and addressed promptly.

This SOP ensures that all orders are handled efficiently, minimizing errors and enhancing customer experience.
